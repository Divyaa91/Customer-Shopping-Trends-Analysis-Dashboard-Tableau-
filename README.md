# Customer Shopping Trends Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)
“An interactive Tableau dashboard for shopping trends and customer demographics, visualizing KPIs like transactions, revenue, and buying behavior by category, mall, gender, and age. Designed for retail analytics and marketing insights.”


# Project Overview

The Shopping Trends Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Tableau project designed to analyze retail and customer shopping behavior. Using real-world sales and customer data, it highlights patterns across shopping categories, demographics, seasons, and payment methods. The dashboard helps businesses understand who their customers are, what they buy, and how they shop, driving more informed marketing and operational decisions.

The project consists of two major dashboards — Overall Shopping Trends and Customer Demographics.

The Overall Shopping Trends dashboard provides a high-level overview of total transactions, unique customers, and total revenue (₹252M). It visualizes sales across categories such as clothing, cosmetics, technology, and food, while also breaking down performance by payment methods, shopping malls, and monthly trends.

The Customer Demographics dashboard focuses on understanding the customer base through gender, age group, and shopping preferences. It showcases metrics like total quantity sold (298.7K units), revenue per customer (₹689.26), and mall preferences by age group.

With interactive filters for gender, mall, category, month, and payment method, users can explore insights dynamically. This dashboard is particularly useful for retail analysts, marketing teams, and business strategists to identify profitable customer groups and plan data-driven promotional campaigns.

The dashboard demonstrates strong skills in data visualization, storytelling, and Tableau dashboard design, turning raw retail data into meaningful insights that enhance customer understanding and retail performance.

# Features

KPI tracking for transactions, customers, and revenue

Gender-wise and age-group shopping trends

Revenue breakdown by product category and shopping mall

Payment method and monthly shopping trend visualization

Interactive filters for detailed exploration

# Tools Used

Tool: Tableau Public

Data Source: Retail shopping dataset (Excel/CSV)

Skills Demonstrated: Data visualization, dashboard design, data-driven storytelling
